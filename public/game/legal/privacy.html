Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CINDERFALL ("the game", "we") handles information when you play. We built the game to respect your privacy: it is designed to run entirely on your device.

1. Information we collect

The game does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, photos, location, or any other personal information. We do not operate servers that receive your gameplay data.

2. Data stored on your device

To let you keep your progress between sessions, the game saves a small amount of data locally on your device only (using browser local storage / the app's local sandbox). This includes:

  • Your operator level, XP and lifetime stats (kills, accuracy, best stage);
  • In-game currency, unlocked items and equipped loadout;
  • Your current mission checkpoint (so you can continue where you left off);
  • Your graphics-quality and audio preferences.

This data never leaves your device. Clearing the app's data, using the in-game "Reset progress" option, or uninstalling the app erases it permanently.

3. No tracking or advertising

The game contains no third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking. We do not use cookies for tracking, and we do not sell or share any information because we do not collect any.

4. Network use

The core game runs offline. If you play the browser version, standard web-server request logs (such as your IP address and browser type) may be recorded by the hosting provider solely to deliver the page and protect against abuse; these logs are not used to identify you and are not linked to your gameplay.

5. Children's privacy

The game is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

6. Purchases

The game's in-game currency and crates are earned through play and have no real-money value. If real-money purchases are added in a future version, any payment is handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play; we never receive or store your payment details.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer contact listed on the game's store page.

CINDERFALL is a work of fiction. All characters, factions and locations are fictional and any resemblance to real persons or organisations is coincidental.
